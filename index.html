About Us

Trent Park lift

Trent Park Engineering is an independent elevator company based in Lagos, Nigeria, offering services across the country. They are committed to providing safe and reliable elevators with a focus on quality, safety, and value. Their engineers are highly trained and experienced. They offer a free, no-obligation inspection of existing lifts and provide a post-assessment report. For new installations, they work closely with clients to understand their needs and design custom solutions.

Trent Park Engineering has a combined professional work experience of 60 years, gained both locally and internationally. Their reputation is growing rapidly with both residential and commercial customers in Nigeria. They are committed to providing professional, ethical, and timely solutions to their customers across the real estate spectrum.

What We Do

Installation (Lifts & Escalators)

Trent Park Engineering provides installation services for lifts and escalators. They offer a wide range of solutions and equipment from leading manufacturers to suit most applications. Their dedicated project management team ensures that projects are delivered on time, within budget, and to the customer's satisfaction.

Lift Modernisation & Refurbishment

They offer lift modernization and refurbishment services to replace obsolete components with modern equivalents, extending the lifespan of existing lifts.

Lift Servicing & Maintenance

They offer a range of maintenance services, from one-off service visits to fully comprehensive contracts. They work closely with customers to develop a service agreement that meets their specific needs.
